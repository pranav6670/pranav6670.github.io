More About me

I am an energetic, highly motivated and a hard-working student. I am highly interested in technologies such as Machine Learning and Deep Learning, web development and deployment. I am passionate to transform any newly learnt skills / technologies into real-time prototypes.Having studied basic courses like Signals & Systems, Digital Signal Processing, Digital Image Processing as an undergrad, laid the groundwork for advanced fields of Computer Science such as Natural Language Processing(NLP), Computer Vision(CV), Speech Processing, etc. These fields have piqued my interest since then, especially Computer Vision.When I am not engrossed in coding or hardware challenges, I play video-games such as CS:GO and outdoor games like football and cricket, also I enjoy playing Tabla with some Indian Classical Music.
